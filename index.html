Hello! Welcome to the Builderment Seeds! Here you can search for any seed to want! This website is made by Space.yg with the help of Human-Crow.

Tips:

  • Click on a seed to copy.
  • Click on a number at the resource colum to get an accurate amount.

Notes:

  • The World Size and Resource Amount are available only to people with advanced world settings. if you do not have advanced world settings, the default for both is 100.
  • To use the World Size and Resource Amount filters, tap on the checkbox to the left od the name of the filter.
  • Choose the resource and it will calculate the maximum amount of that resource at the Resource column.
  • When filtering a raw resource, it gets calculated from the raw resource's column. Other resources get calculated from the Resource column.
  • Since the latest update with the power plants, it is not yet known what is the best seed. The research is still on.
